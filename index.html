About

Adirondack Mail & Shipping Center, located at 334 Cornelia St,
Plattsburgh NY is the go–to resource for packing, shipping and
receiving needs of the residents and businesses of Plattsburgh, NY.
Our team of dedicated, professionally trained experts understands
the meaning of Super-Star Customer Care. We focus on saving you
time and money by ensuring you get the right products and
services at the right price - in a single, quick visit.

We can pack and ship (almost) anything to (almost) anywhere in the world, but
shipping is only one way we can help to make your life easier. In addition to our
pack and ship services, we also offer mailbox rentals and package receiving

services. For savvy Canadian shoppers, buy in the US, benefit from free US
shipping offers when available and avoid brokerage fees entirely.
Simply get it delivered to us, and come pick it up at your
convenience!

Safety, Security & Conveniencei

Receiving mail and packages at your home or business can be
challenging (and potentially risky) for a number of reasons. Here are a
few to consider:

  • Identity thieves can find a treasure trove of information about you by
    stealing letters directly from your home mailbox.
  • UPS, FedEx, DHL and the United States Postal Service often leave
    packages on your porch or doorstep - making it easy for thieves to
    steal them. Package theft like this is one of the fastest growing crimes in
    the US.
  • How many times have you been waiting for an important letter or
    package to find out the driver did not deliver it because he needed your
    signature - but you were at work or out shopping? Remember how
    though it is to get that package?
  • If you travel, what happens to your mail and packages while you’re gone?
  • Do you run a business from your home? A residential address does
    not always give the best of impressions to clients.
    Rent a private mailbox at Adirondack Mail & Shipping Center. Renting a
    private mailbox is the most efficient way to receive your mail and
    packages. Small office and home office business operators have used
    private mailboxes for decades to help manage their business.
    Private citizens use them for safety, security and convenience
